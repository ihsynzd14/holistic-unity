What is Numerology?

Numerology is the study of the vibrational significance of numbers and their influence on human life. Rooted in the teachings of Pythagoras, Numerology holds that every number carries a specific frequency that shapes personality, destiny, and timing. Your most important number is your Life Path Number — calculated from your full birth date — which reveals your core purpose, the natural talents you carry, and the lessons you are here to master. Understanding your Life Path is like discovering the spiritual curriculum of your existence.

Beyond the Life Path, a full numerology reading explores your Destiny Number (your natural talents and career affinities), Soul Urge Number (your deepest inner motivations), Expression Number (how you present yourself to the world), and Personal Year Number (the phase of the 9-year cycle you're currently in). Together, these numbers create a complete energetic portrait that reveals not just who you are, but where you are in your life's journey — and what the next chapter holds.

What Happens in an Online Numerology Session?

01

Share Your Full Birth Information

Provide your full birth date and full birth name to your practitioner. Both pieces of information are essential for calculating your complete numerology profile.

02

Practitioner Calculates Your Numbers

Your verified numerology practitioner calculates your core numbers: Life Path, Destiny, Soul Urge, Expression, and Personal Year. Each number is reduced through a specific formula to reveal its vibrational meaning.

03

In-Depth Interpretation Session

During your HD video call, your practitioner walks you through each number, explaining what your Life Path reveals about your purpose, your Destiny about your natural gifts, and your Personal Year about where you are in your current 9-year cycle.

04

Practical Guidance & Alignment

Your practitioner provides actionable guidance on how to work with your numbers in daily decisions, relationships, and career — helping you align with your numerological blueprint for greater synchronicity and clarity.
