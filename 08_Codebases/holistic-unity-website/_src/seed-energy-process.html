What is SEED – Energy Process®?

SEED – Energy Process® is the energetic practice of inner awakening developed by Gioia Mandriola and Giorgio Alloa, founders of SEED Academy. It is based on the principle that all of existence is one soul, called the Source, and that a part of it chooses to divide and hold within itself the information needed for incarnation (the individual soul). From the individual soul, a fragment separates and chooses to incarnate on the plane of matter (the incarnated soul) to gather experience and contribute to the expansion of the Source.

In a SEED – Energy Process® session, the facilitator works with the recipient to build a direct bridge between the incarnated soul — the part of you that is here in matter, living its blocks, fears, and resistances — and your most authentic part, the individual soul, which holds your fullest potential and a clear map of the path you chose to walk in this life.

This deep connection allows access to the wisdom of the soul, where insights, answers, and visions useful to your own evolution can emerge. Unlike many approaches that work on a single aspect of the human being, SEED supports work across several levels at once — physical, mental, energetic, and spiritual — and many recipients describe the changes as immediate and profound.

The practice makes it possible to work on themes from this life, tracing where they began — whether in this life, a past one, or your family tree. It is accompanied by specific music that shifts according to the energetic field and the purpose of the session: the music does not activate the process, but it supports a state of depth and introspection. SEED is a holistic practice of personal and spiritual growth, and it does not replace psychotherapy or medical care.

How a SEED – Energy Process® Session Works

1

Preparation & Intention

Share what you want to release, transform, or reach — a specific emotional block, an area of life that feels stuck, a recurring pattern, or a wish for more clarity and direction.

2

Energetic Connection

The facilitator connects with the part of your soul that holds the information you need right now to move forward, release, and integrate new parts of yourself — work that touches not only the body or the emotions, but brings change into daily life.

3

Transformation Process

Old memories, unconscious patterns, limiting beliefs, and repressed emotions that keep you from your goal can surface. With the facilitator's guidance they are brought to light and released or integrated, letting you realign with your mission — and with how you think, act, and relate to others.

4

Integration

After the session, through a shared debrief with the facilitator, you gain clarity on what you experienced and receive practical tools to anchor the transformation in the weeks that follow — so you can recognise and step out of the patterns that used to repeat. SEED isn't only a session: it's a tool that helps you become autonomous and in contact with your own inner guidance.
