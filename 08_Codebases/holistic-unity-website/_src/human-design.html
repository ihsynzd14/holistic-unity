What is Human Design?

Human Design is a synthesis of ancient wisdom (Astrology, Kabbalah, I Ching, Chakras) and modern science (quantum physics, biochemistry, genetics) developed by Ra Uru Hu in 1987. It uses your exact birth date, time, and location to create a unique Body Graph — a visual map of your energy centres, channels, and gates. Your Human Design type (Manifestor, Generator, Manifesting Generator, Projector, or Reflector) reveals how you are designed to move through the world, make aligned decisions, and interact with others.

Understanding your Human Design can end the exhaustion of trying to operate against your true nature. Rather than forcing yourself to fit into a mould created by others or outdated beliefs about how you 'should' be, your Human Design reading offers scientific clarity on who you are at a fundamental level — and how to make life decisions that feel effortless, not forced.

What Happens in an Online Human Design Session?

01

Share Your Birth Data

Before your session, provide your exact birth date, time, and location to your analyst. This information is essential for creating an accurate Body Graph.

02

Analyst Prepares Your Chart

Your verified Human Design analyst calculates your complete Body Graph, noting your type, authority, strategy, profile lines, and key energy centre activations.

03

Live Chart Reading

During your HD video session, your analyst walks you through your chart layer by layer — explaining your type and strategy, your inner authority for decision-making, your profile archeypes, and how your key centres influence your life path.

04

Integration & Alignment Guidance

Your analyst provides practical strategies for daily alignment — how to recognize your inner authority signals, when to wait versus act, and how to honour your design in relationships, work, and personal growth.
