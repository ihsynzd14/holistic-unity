What is Astrology?

Astrology is one of humanity's oldest systems of self-knowledge, using the positions of the sun, moon, and planets at the moment of your birth to create a personalised natal chart. Your birth chart is a snapshot of the sky at the exact time and place you were born — a cosmic map of your personality, strengths, challenges, and life themes. Each planet, sign, and house in your chart carries specific meaning, and the aspects (angles) between planets reveal the dynamic interplay of energies shaping your experience.

A professional astrology reading goes far beyond your sun sign — it reveals the full complexity of who you are and why you're here. By exploring your birth chart, a skilled astrologer can illuminate your deepest motivations, relationship patterns, career affinities, and the spiritual lessons woven throughout your life journey.

What Happens in an Online Astrology Reading?

01

Provide Your Birth Details

Share your exact birth date, time, and location with your astrologer. These details are essential for calculating the precise positions of the sun, moon, and planets at your birth.

02

Astrologer Calculates Your Chart

Your verified astrologer creates your complete natal chart, noting the placements of all 10 celestial bodies across the 12 houses and signs, plus key planetary aspects and angles.

03

Live Chart Interpretation

During your HD video session, your astrologer walks you through your sun, moon, rising sign, and the placements of key planets like Mercury, Venus, and Mars. You'll explore how these energies interact through planetary aspects and what they reveal about your character and life path.

04

Current Transits & Future Guidance

Your astrologer discusses how current planetary movements are influencing your life right now, and explores key upcoming transits to help you understand the timing of transformative life phases ahead.
