Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 7, 2026

Holistic Unity ("we," "us," or "our") is operated by StormX Digital. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Holistic Unity (the "App"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account and use our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Profile Information: Profile photo, phone number (optional), and bio details.
  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect precise location data to show you nearby therapists and practitioners.
  • Messages: Content of messages you exchange with therapists through our in-app chat feature.
  • Booking Data: Session bookings, appointment history, and preferences.
  • Billing and Fiscal Information: When you book a paid session, our payment processor (Stripe) collects your legal name, billing address, phone number, and — where applicable — your tax identification number (e.g. Codice Fiscale or P.IVA in Italy, NIF/NIE in Spain, VAT number in the EU/UK, EIN/SSN/ITIN in the US). We store these fields in our database so the therapist can issue you the fiscal document required by their local tax law.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including pages viewed, features used, and session duration.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Connect you with wellness therapists and practitioners.
  • Facilitate session bookings and scheduling.
  • Enable direct messaging between clients and therapists.
  • Show relevant therapists based on your location and preferences.
  • Send booking confirmations, reminders, and service-related notifications.
  • Process payments and enable therapists to issue you the fiscal documents (invoice, receipt, ricevuta, fattura, factura) required by their local tax law.
  • Improve, personalize, and optimize the App experience.
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud-based platform that provides database hosting and authentication services. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest to protect your personal information. Access to user data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • With Therapists (general): When you book a session or send a message, the therapist receives your display name, profile photo, the booking metadata (date, service, duration), and the content of any messages you exchange. This is necessary to provide the service you requested.
  • With Therapists (fiscal data, paid bookings only): After a paid booking is successfully charged, the therapist can access — strictly for the purpose of issuing your fiscal document — the following fields you entered at the Stripe Checkout step: legal name, billing address, phone number, and (where applicable) tax identification number. The therapist does not see your payment-card details, which are handled exclusively by Stripe and never reach our servers. This sharing is grounded in GDPR Article 6(1)(b) (processing necessary for the performance of a contract) and Article 6(1)(c) (compliance with the therapist's legal tax obligation). We log every access to this data in an internal audit trail and apply rate limits to prevent abuse. The therapist is contractually bound (Terms of Service) to use this information solely for invoicing and bookkeeping, and is the data controller for any fiscal document they subsequently issue.
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services that process data on our behalf under strict contractual obligations: Supabase (database and authentication), Stripe (payment processing — collects and stores your billing and fiscal data on their own platform under their Privacy Policy), LiveKit (video session infrastructure), Stream (in-app chat), and Brevo (transactional emails).
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request.
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of our users and the public.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information through the App settings.
  • Disable location services through your device settings.
  • Opt out of non-essential notifications.
  • Request a copy of your data or its deletion by contacting us.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you request account deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have different data protection laws. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App and updating the "Effective Date" above. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
